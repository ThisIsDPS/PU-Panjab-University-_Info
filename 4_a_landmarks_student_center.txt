One of the most popular spots in Chandigarh, StuC is a place for which we all have a special place in our hearts. Offering sumptuous foods
at affordable rates amidst a scenic beauty that is breathtaking.The Student Center, popularly known as StuC, is a major landmark of the city
of Chandigarh and a hub fo student activities. It was inaugurated in 1975. It houses the office of University student's council. There used 
to be a cafeteria called Indian Coffee house with a panoramic view on the top floor of center but now it has shut down its operations.
