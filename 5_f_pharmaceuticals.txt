University Institute of Pharmaceutical Sciences, Panjab University is a premier institute of pharmaceutical education and research assigned 
NIRF Rank 2 in the country. The institute set up in the pre-independent India, at Lahore, in 1944 has a prestigious and long history. 
The institute hosted Pharmaceutical Sciences Oration by a Nobel Laureate, Prof. Ben Feringa and other mega events including global
alumni meet to commemorate its Platinum Jubilee Year in 2019.
