P.U Fine Arts Museum is located at Panjab University, Sector 14, Chandigarh, 160014, On display are some of the finest artists and their work. It 
has a large collection of works of contemporary Indian art, including paintings, sculptures, graphics and drawings by artists such as Jamini Roy, 
M.F. Husain, Satish Gujral, J. Swaminathan, Ram Kumar, A. Ramachandran, K.G. Subramanyan, Vivan Sundaram, Bhupen Khakhar, Dhanraj Bhagat, Arpana 
Caur, Shiv Singh etc.
